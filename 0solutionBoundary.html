Chess board

  • Download the HTML, CSS, and images
  • Select a chess piece
  • Final goal: Use keyboard to move piece around board

First Goal

As a user, select a chess piece to highlight the square with a red outline.

As a dev, loop through all the chess pieces and bind a click event. Handle the click by applying a CSS rule of border: red solid 2px

Second Goal

As a dev, bind the arrow keys to move the selected chess piece on the page
